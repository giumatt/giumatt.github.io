## About the rice

Recently, I made this rice for my Arch Linux (and later Artix Linux) machine. I wanted to make this as simple ad it possible just to improve productivity.
I choose the **_BSPWM_** window manager just because I'm comfy with that and also, just like other WM, is highly customizable. The presence of the **_sxhkd_** daemon to control al the keybinds is just perfect: you have two config files, one for the BSPWM behavior and the other to set all the keybinds.
Also, I choose the Catppuccin theme with the mocha flavor because it is a warm violet that just matches with the background colors.
